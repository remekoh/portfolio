War Official

Clothing line for the modern individual

The Client

War Official is a clothing line created by Charles Johnson - who also handles the design and manufacturing of the products. The line draws it's inspiration from the styles in the 1979 classic: The Warriors.

My Role

Charles wanted a site that would reflect his brand: clean & modern; and most importantly a secure platform that will handle all the behind-the-scenes e-commerce heavy lifting.

Based on the specification I received from the client, I decided that the best approach would be to use Shopify, an e-commerce platform trusted by over 200,000 merchants worldwide.

The client also requested a unique lookbook; this is one area where Shopify doesn't have an in-house solution, so I decided to use a third-party solution: Galleria - a JavaScript image gallery framework.
