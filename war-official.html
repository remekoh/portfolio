About

Howdy! I'm a Consultant Front-end Developer creative problem solver with a passion for building intuitive products. I am highly resourceful, have exceptional attention to detail and relish the challenges presented by complex projects.

One of the things I love about what I do - which I also consider the most important factor in my work - is creating an excellent user experience. Some of my previous clients include Houston Methodist, Houston Independent School District, Houston Counseling and Mediation Center, Beacon Limo, and Manifield Solicitors among others.

In addition to web development, I enjoy playing tennis and soccer: I'm a passionate supporter of Manchester United Football Club.

Solutions I provide include:

User Research

I investigate the impact of your (product) design on intended audience. This process varies widely depending on the type of site, system or app you are developing.

UI/UX Design

I create an enabling environment for your users to acheive their goals through your digital products, ensuring an intuitive, impactful, and even delightful experience.

Code Development

I ensure your final product not only meets but exceeds the current industry standard using modern web technologies with strict adherence to best practices.
